System Logic v1.0
ℹ️ Mirrors the OrderBlockDetector and SignalEngine rules from your system. Answer each question to validate an OB before entering manually.
Question 1 of 4
Did the impulse break a prior swing high/low?
System check: _is_impulse() verifies the close of bar i+n_imp exceeds the prior swing high (bullish) or low (bearish) within a lookback of 5 bars. This is the BOS (Break of Structure) requirement.
Question 2 of 4
Is this the LAST opposing candle before the impulse?
System logic: walks backward from the impulse start (for j in range(i, i-lb-1, -1)) and selects the first bearish candle (for Bullish OB) or first bullish candle (for Bearish OB). Only the last opposing candle qualifies.
Question 3 of 4
Has this OB been tapped / mitigated before?
System: _mark_mitigated() — Bullish OB is invalidated when price closes below OB bottom; Bearish OB when price closes above OB top. Once mitigated, active=False. A tapped OB loses its institutional interest.
Question 4 of 4
Is the impulse at least 2–3 strong consecutive candles?
System config: impulse_candles=3. All 3 candles must close in the same direction (all_same check). Strong candles = large bodies, minimal wicks. ATR filter also validates volatility: atr_now ≥ 0.5 × atr_mean.
